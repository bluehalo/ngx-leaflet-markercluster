zoomToShowLayer — Zone.js Timing

MarkerClusterGroup.zoomToShowLayer(marker, callback) zooms and pans until the target marker is visible, then fires a callback. When called inside Angular's zone, zone.js patches requestAnimationFrame and wraps every animation frame in a zone task that triggers change detection. This creates contention with leaflet.markercluster's icon-update timing, causing the callback to be silently dropped.

The target is NYC (40.71, -74.00), clustered with 199 other nearby markers. Run Trials executes 10 attempts inside the zone, each with a RAF pressure loop to amplify the timing race. Fixed should always succeed.
